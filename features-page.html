Feature Highlights

SAFE Locker is an agile and efficient loan management software that automates the post-disbursement activities of a loan life cycle to cater the needs of lending institutions.

Digitized End-to-End System

Complete digitization of locker management system with seamless automation and real-time tracking capabilities.

Seamless Integration

Effortless software integration and configuration with existing banking infrastructure and third-party systems.

Flexible Inventory System

Advanced locker inventory management with dynamic allocation and smart space optimization algorithms.

Biometric Authentication

Secure locker access through advanced biometric authentication and comprehensive trail management.

Dynamic Dashboard

Comprehensive dashboard with real-time locker status, operations monitoring, payment tracking, and historical analytics.

Instant Notifications

Smart notification system providing instant alerts for rent collections, arrears, and critical operational events.
